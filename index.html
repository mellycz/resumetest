Coding, Newsvoo and Me

I first began learning to code when I was in Year 9 as one of my elective subjects.  Soon I found myself sucked into the programming wonderland.  Even though what I learnt was really basic at the time, but I really enjoyed the feeling when I was able to turn something of my imagination into reality through coding.


Then in the beginning of 2015, I was introduced to Meteor by chance.  At first I found it hard to wrap my head around the concept that it was built on.  But once I practised a little while, I realised that it is actually one of the best tools I have ever used to build Javascript websites and apps.


I decided to have a go at programming a news website from scratch utilising my newly acquired Meteor knowledge for practise.  So this is how I came to be one of the two developers of the site Newsvoo.com.


Newsvoo.com officially went online at the start of 2016.  Even though we started small, but the number of readers is increasing every day, and they are from all corners of the planet.  We also have diligent reporters working across the globe, writing articles and conjuring reports that matters to the Chinese people 7 days a week.


It is my hope that I can go as far as I can on the coding path, but aside from this, I'm also enthusiastic about leadership, public speaking, foreign affairs and politics.  Please click on my resume to learn more about me.
